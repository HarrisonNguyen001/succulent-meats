Menu

  • Crested Butte

    The Crested Butte is a thick slab of a succulent pig. It can come however you want it cooked.

  • Filet Mignon

    The Filet Mignon is like a steak, but of better succulent quality. It comes cooked medium well, unless specified otherwise.

  • Robuchon au Dôme

    The Robuchon au Dôme is three thick slabs of a succulent meat that the chefs choose not to give up. This very tasty meat can come however you want it cooked.

  • Chicken Dinner

    The Chicken dinner is one of the original Succulent Meats meals. The butcher that created this wanted someone casual, but to also make it look nice.

  • Fagers Island

    Fagers Island is the only fish meal the Succulent Meats franshise serves. However, this is the most succulent fish you will ever taste. Taken straight out of the coast of Japan, this is an authentic and succulent meal.

  • Suckling Pig Dish

    The Suckling Pig Dish can serve up to four people, and is great for parties! The pig's are home grown from our local ranch and are raised to be the most succulent pigs ever.

  • Succulent Sausages

    This is also a classic dish. These are great for the kids!

  • Fine Dining Wings

    The Fine Dining Wings are something casual, but classy. They're like a food you'd serve at a Superbowl party, but for very rich, classy people that eat pizza with a fork.

  • Duck Meatballs

    The Duck Meatballs are from the most succulent of ducks. They can be ordered in small (3) medium (7) or large (12) sizes.
